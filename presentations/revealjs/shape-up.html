Epilog - Common Agile Failures

  • Focus on 100% resource utilization
  • Lack of ownership
  • Ineffective or absent collaboration
  • Focusing on high utilization is perhaps the single biggest mistake you can make - neutralizes expertise
  • You can tell you have a problem when even the decision makers won't take responsibility for a decision
  • And the people doign the degrade to just "doing what they are told"
  • Lack of ownership especially with respect to technical debt
  • Us vs. them mentality, unidirectional relationships

The Whole Point

  • Build the right thing

  • If you want to go faster, increase quality ahead of effort

Pitch

A written proposal expressing a problem area, solution parameters, justification, and boundaries
  • Problem/justification are key aspects of the pitch
  • Want to mention rabbit holes and no-gos as well.

NOTE: Anyone can write a pitch!

Man, I hope everyone has seen Ratatoille

Appetite

Budget, in the sense of how much "product" is willing to spend on a specific pitch. Typically expressed as either a "Small Batch" of 2 weeks or a "Big Batch" of 6 weeks.

Negotiation

Discussion, between product, engineering, design, and test, to decide a preferred implementation within the appetite.
